 No.K-14, LG2 Floor, B Tower, Parkview Green, No. 9 Dongdaqiao Road, Chaoyang District, Beijing 《小徑分岔的花園》是來自中央美術學院的康蕾老師和研究生們共同完成的一次聯展，也是師生合作的創作教學實踐。參展藝術家盡管都是中央美院油畫系畢業和在讀的研究生，但從年齡到國際、地域、文化背景卻都不盡相同，這也是展覽作品面貌各異的前提。在康蕾老師關注於時間和距離、客觀和真實的可能性生發的基調下，同學們用各自的藝術語言面對自然、城市生存空間問題展開探討和創作。 在康蕾的藝術世界裡，植物和生命是最直觀的表達，她擅長用作品作為平台來營造各種對話。從「向日葵」到「仙人掌」，再到代表世界的「樂園」，康蕾不斷地嘗試著完善對空間、時間和心理上的距離的把控，她坦然面對人性的弱點，並融入在對植物的刻畫裡，她在抽象的空間和不確定的時間裏留下自我的定格。繁複而散落的印象回憶在坦培拉的介質裡經歷沈澱。 The Garden of Forking Paths is a group exhibition which sees the participation of Teacher Kang Lei and graduate students from the CAFA. It is also the creative teaching practice of teacher-student cooperation.Although the participating artists are all students in the Oil Painting Department of the CAFA, they are all different in terms of age , regional and background. This is also the premise of the multifaceted character of the exhibition. Through the concept elaborated by Kang Lei focusing issues of time, distance, objective and real possibilities, the students explore the theme of nature and urban living space. in their own artistic language. In Kang Lei's art world, plants and life are the most intuitive forms of expression. She is good at using the concept of dialogue as the starting point for her work. From "sunflower" to "cactus" to "paradise", Kang Lei is constantly investigating the possibility of controlling the distance between space and time. She addresses the theme of the weakness of human nature and integrates it into the artistic representation of plants. She operates with abstract forms and uncertain time frame to create frozen images.
