 No.K-14, LG2 Floor, B Tower, Parkview Green, No. 9 Dongdaqiao Road, Chaoyang District, Beijing</activity_address_LxKyMA7eXL> 「觸感」是黃幾繪畫創作中持續關注的概念，這個概念源於傳統書法的拓片及其拓碑過程。藝術家把書法中的「拓」與繪畫中的「如實描繪」，理解為對萬物的「觸感」呈現。中國傳統的拓印技術在某種程度上是一種對於痕跡和歲月的記錄。「痕跡」是無數代人的共同記憶。 關於「觸覺」概念展開的一系列創作的邏輯與脈絡，涉及到創作動機與工作方法等。黃幾把它做成手稿的模樣，在圖示與文本的敘述中，將自我的意識徐徐呈現。《觸覺自述》描述的是不同的感官和意識層面的創作過程。 The tactile feeling is a concept that Huang Ji often addresses in his painterly creation. This concept comes from the rubbing of traditional calligraphy and its materialization. He understands the concept of "extension" in calligraphy and the "true depiction" in painting as a way of perceiving and touching all the things. Chinese traditional rubbing technique represents a way to record the traces of time. These traces belong to the collective memory.  This is the logic and the concept behind the idea of “touch”. The Tactile Self-Report describes the process of intervention and feedback of different perceptions during my creative process.
