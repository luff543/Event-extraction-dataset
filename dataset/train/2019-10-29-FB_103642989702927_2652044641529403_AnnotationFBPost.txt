 ＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿What is a living-well life? 
 According to a book “Twelve By Twelve”, Aymara people living in Bolivia do not seek to enrich the materialistic life. They prefer to “live well”, not “live better”, which means friends, family, health, fresh air and water, enough food and peace.
