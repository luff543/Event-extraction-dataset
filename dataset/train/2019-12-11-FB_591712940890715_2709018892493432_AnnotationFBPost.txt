 “CODE2040”, a massive online shooter game developed by WANIN Games, based on the Remains of the 13 Levels of Shuinandong in Taiwan, announced today that it will be hosting a cross-industry collaboration with the well-known Taiwanese airsoft gun manufacturer VegaForceCompany (from here on be referred to as VFC) to implant the Samurai Edge assault rifle into the actual game, and the goal is to brings players a brand new shooting experience! Producer Lin Jingmin said that he is a hardcore airsoft player, in addition to strengthening the idea of it being made purely in Taiwan, he hopes that he can with mutual cooperation reach more players who love military, weapons and outdoor activities, so that more people can learn about VFC, join "CODE2040" and understand the R & D capabilities of various industries in Taiwan! In addition, at the 2019 MOA Taiwan International Military / Outdoor / Airsoft Gun Supplies Exhibition "CODE2040" will participating at the VFC exhibition area. Players can get the "CODE2040" Spec Op Velcro armband and the exquisite gifts provided by VFC if they complete the designated tasks at the venue.
