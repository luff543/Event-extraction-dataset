 【AKI Gallery Taipei】
 Artist Nikos ASLANIDIS is presenting his new works at AKI Gallery until December 15, 2019 in a group exhibition “Europe Calling—A Collection of Paintings” together with the other 12 brilliant artists. Nikos ASLANIDIS is expert in creating figurative paintings. He pushes his limit to achieve further. He outlines the images with fluid paints to create religious atmosphere. People can’t stop staring at his paintings because of his strong sense of momentum and the way he arranges hidden details. As for Nikos, painting is like an endless process. During the process, personalities of all the characters appear when the layers of colors are obliterated.  Artworks of Nikos ASLANIDIS have been published in Wall Street Journal, attracting attention of collectors in Europe and Asia. He’s attended many group exhibitions in Europe such as Athens, Cologne, Dusseldorf and Vienna and won various awards, including the Boutari Nouveau competition, Fresco, Saulty Residence and the Deste Foundation. AKI Gallery would like to invite you to join the show ‘Europe Calling—A Collection of Paintings’. Step on this spectacular journey with us from Nov. 23 to Dec. 15.  ➤➤ Read more
