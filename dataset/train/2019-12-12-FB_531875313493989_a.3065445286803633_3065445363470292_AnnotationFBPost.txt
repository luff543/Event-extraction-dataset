 To The Moon - LIU KUO-SUNG 展出現代水墨先鋒畫家劉國松作品64件，展覽依題材區分為狂草抽象、太空、水拓，漬墨與西藏組曲等系列;展品年代自1964年迄今，縱跨半個世紀，不僅是藝術家個人創作歷程 之精微再現，亦可視為臺灣美術發展的歷史性回顧事件之一。⋯⋯ The To the Moon: Liu Kuo-sung exhibition consists of 64 works by the avant garde contemporary ink painter Liu Kuo-sung. The works on display are classified by subject matter into “Calligraphic Abstraction,” “Space,” ” Water Rubbing,” ” Steeped Ink,” and “Tibetan Suite,” series; the dates these works span the half-century period from 1964 to the present. The exhibition not only provides an in-depth look at the artist’s personal creative history, but can be also be seen as historical retrospective of the development of art in Taiwan. 高雄市立美術館  Kaohsiung Museum of Fine Arts
