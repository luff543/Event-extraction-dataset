Exhibition Rearrangement 

就在藝術空間籌備歷時一年的香港巴塞爾藝術展參展計畫，一週前突然面臨了巨大的考驗及變畫，本月初載運作品的貨輪發生了船難事故，目前仍擱淺於台灣石門外海。因應這樣的突發狀況我們很遺憾地將無法如期呈現部分的原定作品，包含陳松志展前極受矚目以玻璃搭建的大型裝置作品。

儘管距離展會僅一週的時間，我們仍迅速穩住腳步用心地規劃接下來的展出。今年是就在藝術空間第三度參與香港巴塞爾藝術展，也是首次進入重要的主展區「藝廊薈萃（Galleries）」，將與世界的頂級畫廊佩斯(PACE)、SCAI、David Zwirner等等並列同一展區。我們本次維持展出兩位優秀的代理藝術家陳松志以及王福瑞。

藝術家陳松志將以靈活的現地製作方式來呈現今年最新空間裝置作品《無題2016》，於寬七公尺的牆面藉以人造塑料、水、織品以及印刷紙張等饒富集體記憶的現成物作為生命多重隱喻的象徵。另一件作品為陳松志2014年於就在藝術空間個展極受好評的作品《別境系列-航》，近期也受邀於高雄市立美術館展出，是以棉襪、印刷紙，碎布及地毯組合而成的造型裝置。

而藝術家王福瑞則維持原定計畫，將展出重要作品《聲點》聲音空間裝置，透過聲音的創作來傳達當下生活狀態與運算機器間的密切關係；以及另一件作品《聲影》，使用各種音頻的變化或混頻來產生各種影像的可能性。

The exhibition Project Fulfill Art Space has been on preparation for Art Basel Hong Kong 2016, is now facing a major test and change due to the shipwreck of the freighter that carries the artworks. Unfortunately, the freighter is still stranded in the offshore of ShiMen, Taiwan. In response to the accident, we are sorry to announce that Project Fulfill Art Space is not showing part of the original content and artworks of artist CHEN Sung-Chih, including the eminence glass installation. 

Although it is a week till Art Basel Hong Kong 2016, we stand firm and elaborate on coordinating the up coming show. It is the third year of participating Art Basel – Hong Kong, and it is the first time we participate in the exhibition sector “Galleries” of Art Basel – Hong Kong 2016. We will be seen with all the top galleries around the world, including PACE, SCAI and David Zwirner. We will be presenting two of our contracted renowned artists WANG Fujui and CHEN Sung-Chih.

CHEN Sung-Chih’s past works were all based on this idea, using the language of the material itself to present the sensitive segments of the life that are often ignored. Worth mentioning, the artist is bringing the newest on-site installation artwork of the year, Untitled 2016 to the exhibition. Starts with a 7 meter wide wall, Untitled 2016 used materials rich in collective memories, such as artificial plastic, liquid, fabric, and printing paper, as the multiple metaphors of life.

The pioneer of Taiwan sound arts WANG Fujui will present one of the most important pieces Sound Dots in the booth. This sound installation delivers the message that addresses the relationship of contemporary life and computing machines.In Seeing Sound, the artist tries to create various possibilities for images through frequency variation or mixing. Accordingly, it is a visual installation that produces images with inaudible sound.
