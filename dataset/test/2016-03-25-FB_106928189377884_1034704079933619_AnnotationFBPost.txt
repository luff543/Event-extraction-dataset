[Exhibition | Beijing | Abstraction × Geometry: Su Yi & Tang Mingwei]

如果說藝術也有內在規律，那抽象和幾何，可以說是其中最明顯的規則。蘇藝理性地推敲線、面位置，將結構與色彩結合在一起，在平面空間中尋求純視覺上的平衡美感；唐明偉用密密麻麻的橫豎線條形成一種界定的形式，讓那橫豎線就像無數的座標。藝術真的只是感性而沒有規則嗎？數學、物理就只有理性的公式嗎？快到北京亞洲藝術中心發現它們的關係吧。

Is there any immanent rule in art? Probably abstraction and geometry are the most conspicuous ones. Su Yi's way of creation is to rationally observe the juxtaposition of line and plane and then combine the structure and color, pursuing a balanced aesthetics in view of plane space in a visual sense. Tang Mingwei's rule is that he builds up a defined form by means of numerous horizontal and vertical lines, those lines are somewhat like a great number of a coordinates. Is art mere emotionality without rules? Are mathematics and physics mere rationality and formulas? Come join us at Asia Art Center in Beijing to find out their connection.
