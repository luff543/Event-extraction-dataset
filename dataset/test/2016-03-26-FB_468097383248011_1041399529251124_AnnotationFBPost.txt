Hyperplasia ∞ Mutation is the solo exhibition of British animation wizard, Cyriak Harris. The exhibition includes his 8 representative works since 2007. These surreal and humorous animations  combine the sound effect of 8-bit computer games and edited images , bringing an infinite imaginative space to audience and creating tens of millions views on YouTube. When passing by the MOCA LED TV Wall,the dramatic plots would guide you into Cyriak’s animated world of magical realism!
