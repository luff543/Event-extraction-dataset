【228｜展覽活動】
[ 228 Exhibition and Events ]
近年來轉型正義的提倡與推動、公民意識抬頭，讓大家更加積極也有機會了解台灣的歷史與真相，對此藝術家則是以各異的形式反思和回應，甚或試圖弭平傷痛。在和平紀念日的今天，也有許多相關特展在各地陸續展開，趁著休假去找出歷史的真相吧！
更多活動：http://www.228.org.tw/info.aspx?v=98D5364BE631EE18
　
Recently, the issue of transitional justice has been promoted, and civic awareness aroused, providing people lots of opportunities to get in touch with the history and facts of Taiwan. Through various forms, artists try to introspect and response to these issues and materials, and even to cease the pain as well as bring comfort. Today is the 228 Memorial Day, many exhibitions are held around the country. How about going out to explore the truth of history?
View more: http://www.228.org.tw/info.aspx?v=98D5364BE631EE18
