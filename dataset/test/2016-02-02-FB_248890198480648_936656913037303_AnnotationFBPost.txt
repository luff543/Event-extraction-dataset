❤❤策展理念
蕭壠文化園區訂於2016年2月8日至2月14日舉辦「2016甜吻吻—蕭壠國際當代藝術節」，希冀加速蕭壠糖業文化與當代藝術之接軌。
　
為行銷「2016 甜吻吻—蕭?國際當代藝術節」，活動期間並同步推出「幸福e記憶」特展，強化糖業發展與蕭?文化園區的連結，邀請民眾一同了解糖的身世與生活的甜蜜回憶！
　
本展依「幸福的記憶」規劃三個主要子題：「海洋記憶裡的糖」、「家庭記憶裡的糖」與「臺灣文學裡的糖」。「海洋記憶裡的糖」以臺灣為輻輳，延伸了解臺灣鄰國的糖文化；「家庭記憶裡的糖」則以世代記憶的時間為層，縱向的觀看臺灣糖文化的更迭；「臺灣文學裡的糖」則從藝術文學的角度，觀看糖品的時代氛圍及記憶。

Sweet Memories
2016/02/08-2016/04/10
Curator: Hsu Yuan- Ta
　　
“Sweet Memories- Soulangh International Contemporary Art Festival 2016” starts from February 8th to February 14th, 2016. The special exhibition, “Sweet Memories”, connects the history of sugar industry and contemporary art. Stories of sugar will draw you into the historical background of sugar industry and bring you sweet memories in your life.
　　
There are three parts: “sea voyages of sugar”, “sugar in household memories”, and “sugar in Taiwanese literature”. “Sea voyages of sugar” explores Taiwan’s sugar export history and sugar cultures in its neighboring countries. “Sugar in household memories” displays the transformation of Taiwan’s sugar culture through the layers of different generations. “Sugar in Taiwanese literature” shows the atmosphere and memories of various eras from an artistic perspective.
