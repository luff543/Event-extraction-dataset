白爾樂（1975）於德國柏林藝術大學取得碩士學位，並且於2012年獲得歷史悠久的美茵茨藝術獎。白爾樂以流動的視角重組日常所見的生活場景，藉景物的錯位、扭曲，營造出藝術家賦與主觀詮釋的強烈臨場感。有別於超現實主義光怪陸離的夢境，白爾樂的場景呼應了生活中親身經歷的片段，卻又與全紀實的視覺經驗保持了一定的距離，創造出一種熟悉而與印象若即若離的詩性一隅。

Sabine Beyerle(1975-), winner of Mainz Art Award in 2012, studied at the graduate school of Universität der Künste Berlin. She reorganises the scenes from her everyday life with flowing perspective. By twisting and distorting the images, she creates a strong sense of being on site out of her subjectivity. Beyele's work coordinates with her life experience while keeping a certain distance from the real world. This differentiates itself from the dreamlike surrealistic scene and creates a poetic corner derived from a deja vu and loose reminiscence.
