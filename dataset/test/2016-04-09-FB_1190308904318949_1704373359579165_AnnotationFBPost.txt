Don’t miss this cool exhibition titled Kill Me or Change by New York based artist Chin-Chih Yang at Museum of Contemporary Art (MOCA) Taipei between now and June 1, 2016. Yang uses recycled disposables such as aluminum cans, snack packaging, and electronic materials to create interactive installations with a powerful environmental protection message. Admission is free. MOCA is just 12 minutes’ walk from amba Taipei Zhongshan hotel.
