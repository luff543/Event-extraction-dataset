畫家Dominika自述：
我的名字是Dominika  Dickerson,來自波蘭的畫家。由於老公是個機師，我跟著他在全世界旅遊後，最近在台灣定居。對於藝術的熱誠從兒時便開始萌芽，其也隨著年紀的增長不斷的改變及增深。而在繪畫的旅途上，我嘗試過不同畫風、多種技法及各式媒材（包括油畫及壓克力顏料），而出乎意料的，我的風格逐漸專精於墨，guash,及水彩。原先我會使用各種顏料，但我體會到當顏色回到黑白的時候，我更能專注於繪畫的技巧上。我享受於創作黑白畫作，唯有其他顏色被除去的時候，我們才能專心於光影效果及細節的變化，而不被五顏六色所干涉。我想繪畫的快樂，正來自對於這樣發掘自己畫作的真諦。
我的畫作主要來自於我跟著丈夫於全世界飛行時所見所聞，從斐濟的村莊到義大利的街景都是我畫作的範圍。盼客人能隨著我的畫作，跟我探索世界的各個角落。

"My name is Dominika Dickerson and I'm an artist from Poland. I recently relocated to Taipei following my husband, who was offered a position in Taiwan. My passion for painting started when I was a little girl, and gradually changed (as it always does) with time. Through the years I tried different paints, techniques and medias (including oils and acrylics), but unexpectedly my art has evolved in a direction of ink, guash and waterlolour. At first I was using the whole palette of colours, but I realized that there is much more subtlety to technique when one only uses black. I enjoy making black & white pieces because once the colours are removed, our attention is drawn to lighting and details, that would otherwise pass unnoticed. I find it wonderful - the idea of discovering a painting. 

I usually paint places and people I see and meet while travelling with my husband. Hence my pieces show all from a Fijian village to an Italian street view."
