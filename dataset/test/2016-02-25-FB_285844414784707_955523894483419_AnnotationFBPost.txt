European Summer Program in FRANCE  (July 2016)

研習時間：<activity_date_start_LxKyMA7eXL>2016年06月28日</activity_date_start_LxKyMA7eXL>至<activity_date_end_LxKyMA7eXL>07月23日</activity_date_end_LxKyMA7eXL>(出發時間以實際機票日期為準)
Time: 28 June – 23 July 2016

研習地點：文藻姐妹校─<activity_loc_LxKyMA7eXL>法國里爾天主教大學</activity_loc_LxKyMA7eXL>
Place: Lille (France) Catholic University of Lille

研習對象：(一)  年滿18歲對進修法文、對法國文化有興趣或希望瞭解歐盟背景之本校同學。

Participants: From beginners to advanced students in French.

課程內容：
Courses:  
- Intensive French Language Program (small classes) (48hours – 4 ECTS credits) 
- Elective courses (taught in French or in English) (40 hours – 6ECTS credits)

研習費用：
(一) 2,320歐元(約新臺幣85,000元)
以上費用僅為研習營學費、行政規費、住宿費(學校宿舍單人房)、食費(週一至週五早 午餐)、交通費(交通卡)、五次團體晚餐、兩次校外旅遊費用(布魯塞爾及布魯日)、使用里爾天主教大學校內相關設施費用、國際學生文化活動。
(一) 至少75歐元之醫療保險 (約新臺幣2,500元)。
(二) 須再自行加上來回機票、簽證、保險等費用。
Price: 
- 2,320 Euros (+/- 85,000 NT$) (including tuition fees, administration fee, accommodation in single rooms, breakfast (weekdays), Meal pass for lunches and evening in the cafeteria, public transport pass, 2 Fieldtrips to Brussels and Bruges, 5 group dinners, access to university facilities, International Student Night, Movie nights) 
- + 75 Euros (+/- 2500 NT$) (Health insurance) 
- + flight ticket (booked by the participant)

如去年2015年法國里爾暑假研習團，今年2016年七月一樣由羅文笙Prof. Vincent ROLLET老師(法國語文系老師)作為隨團老師，帶領學生前往法國里爾作暑期研習，同時也會協助此團學生處理行前準備工作及註冊ESP暑假研習課程。

Like last year, this year (July 2016), Prof. Vincent ROLLET (French Department) will lead the group of students who plan to go to Lille to participate to this program and will help them to prepare administrative documents to register to the ESP. 

如果有任何問題，歡迎隨時Email詢問隨團 羅文笙 老師。
